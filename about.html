Quick and Simple Bio About Me


My name is Ebunoluwa Blessing Adebesin, but most times I go by Ebun or Blessing. I am a Recent College Graduate! I majored in Computer science with a minor in Mathematics at Huston Tillotson University, which is the first HBCU in Austin, Texas. My interests include software engineering, problem solving, data analysis, and management. Looking for a full-time position at a company where I can use my knowledge to solve real world problems, enhance the company's products and services, and further develop my abilities to deliver the best of the best. I love Computer Science and the broad opportunities it has to offer! Long-term, I hope to use my degree, skills and experiences to bring technology closer to the less priviledge.

My favorite thing about studying Computer Science is the vast opportunities and careers in the field. My goal is definitely to work in a field that allows me to actively inact changes, be it just in bug fixes or designing a website or software, I want to work where I see my work and the effects it has on the company and the customers.
